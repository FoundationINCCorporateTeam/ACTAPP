Frequently Asked Questions

Is ACT AI Tutor free?
Yes! ACT AI Tutor is completely free to use. You get unlimited access to all features including AI lessons, quizzes, practice tests, and the AI tutor chat.
How accurate is the AI tutor?
Our AI tutor uses state-of-the-art language models that are highly accurate for ACT-related content. However, like any AI system, it may occasionally make mistakes. We recommend verifying important information, especially for official test dates or college admission requirements.
Can I use this on mobile?
Yes! ACT AI Tutor is fully responsive and works great on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.
How do I improve my score?
  1. Take practice quizzes regularly to identify weak areas
  2. Generate lessons on topics you struggle with
  3. Use the AI tutor to get explanations for concepts you don't understand
  4. Create and follow a study plan
  5. Take full practice tests to build stamina and time management skills
  6. Review your mistakes and understand why answers are correct
How long should I study for the ACT?
Most students benefit from 2-3 months of focused preparation. However, this varies based on your starting score, target score, and available study time. Use our study plan generator to get a personalized schedule based on your specific situation.
What AI models are available?
We offer 13 different AI models including DeepSeek V3.2 (recommended), GLM 4.7 Thinking, Llama 4 Maverick, Mistral Large 3, and more. Different models have different strengths - feel free to experiment!
